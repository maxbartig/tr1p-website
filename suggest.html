Tell us what TR1P should build next.

Have an idea for better trip insights, smarter route comparisons, cleaner sharing, or new charts? Send it here and we will use it to shape future TR1P updates.

Suggest a feature

The more specific the idea, the more useful it is. Tell us what problem it solves, who it helps, and how you imagine it working inside the app.

Good suggestions include

  • The problem you are trying to solve
  • What part of TR1P the idea improves
  • How often you would use it
  • What the ideal screen or workflow would feel like

Popular idea areas

  • Deeper route comparison and time saved views
  • More charts, streaks, and historical insights
  • Smarter incident detection and trip summaries
  • Better sharing, exporting, and premium features
